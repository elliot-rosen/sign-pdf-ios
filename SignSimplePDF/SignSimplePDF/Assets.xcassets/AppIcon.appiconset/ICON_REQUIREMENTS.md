- 60x60 (20@3x)
- 58x58 (29@2x)
- 87x87 (29@3x)
- 80x80 (40@2x)
- 120x120 (40@3x)
- 120x120 (60@2x)
- 180x180 (60@3x)

## iPad Icons
- 20x20 (20@1x)
- 40x40 (20@2x)
- 29x29 (29@1x)
- 58x58 (29@2x)
- 40x40 (40@1x)
- 80x80 (40@2x)
- 152x152 (76@2x)
- 167x167 (83.5@2x)

## App Store Icon
- 1024x1024 (iOS Marketing)

Total: 17 icon files needed

## Icon Design Guidelines
- Simple, recognizable design
- No transparency
- No rounded corners (iOS applies them)
- High contrast
- Works well on various backgrounds
